Dragon Squadron SURFEX

Exercise Order · Hong Kong Squadron · Above-water warfare serial

To: The Commanding Officer, HMS Peacock.

You are required and directed to proceed in company with units of Dragon Squadron for the conduct of a competitive surface exercise in Hong Kong waters. The object of the serial is to exercise judgement, manoeuvre, identification and fire-control discipline in a congested littoral environment.

The area contains neutral merchant, fishing and sailing traffic. You are to maintain safe navigation, avoid all land and shoal danger, and engage only a correctly identified opposing Peacock-class patrol vessel. Any engagement of neutral traffic, departure from the exercise area, or navigation hazard will constitute failure of the serial.

Exercise control will provide periodic tactical updates. Your radar team, lookout and 76mm crew are available for tasking. Reports are to be assessed by you as commanding officer. You are to act on judgement, not assumption.

Intent: locate, identify and engage the opposing patrol vessels while preserving freedom of manoeuvre and avoiding civilian interference.

By order
For Officer Commanding, Dragon Squadron
